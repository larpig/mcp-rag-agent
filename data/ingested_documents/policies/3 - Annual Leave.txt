UK Employees:

Full-time employees receive 25 days of paid annual leave per year.
Leave requests should be submitted at least two weeks in advance.
Unused leave cannot be carried over except in exceptional circumstances.

EU Employees:

Full-time employees receive 30 days of paid annual leave per year.
Leave requests should be submitted at least four weeks in advance.
Up to 5 days unused leave can be carried over.

US Employees:

Full-time employees receive 15 days of paid annual leave per year.
Leave requests should be submitted at least one week in advance.
Unused leave cannot be carried over.
